Greenwich Mirrors

Architectural Scale Installation Proposal.

Greenwich Mirrors is an urban-scale installation along the River Thames in London that responds to the loss of historic views disrupted by luxury developments near the Canary Wharf financial district. The project reimagines a neglected gathering space through mobile mirrors that revive displaced vistas and invite public interaction. Activated by tides and manual adjustment, the mirrors act as both optical devices and monuments, blending community memory with the language of social infrastructure.
