Cast of Characters

Extruded Polystyrene Sculptures.

Cast of Characters is a series of polystyrene sculptures cut from large blocks using robotic hot-wire fabrication. The process yields sharp, intricate profiles that transform a familiar industrial material into unexpected forms. The work emphasizes a tension between precision cutting and irregular figuration, presenting the foam not as disposable matter but as a medium for sculptural inquiry. The result is a play between material banality and formal interest, prompting viewers to reconsider the possibilities of process, surface, and scale.

In collaboration with Anass Benachmi and David Eskenazi

A robotic arm with a hot wire cutter mounted on it was used to cut the sculptures.
