Twisted Torus

Mixed-media sculptures based on geometric projection and distortion.

Twisted Torus is a series of sculptural studies investigating the geometry of the torus through digital modeling and physical fabrication. Twisting the surface and reshaping the profile generates forms where a complex interior emerges from a distorted but continuous exterior shell. Using curved projection techniques, the exterior is pierced to reveal part of the interior.
